Connecting the brave with the loyal

JC

Labrador Retriever | Age 5 | Female

JC is our social spark plug. She has a sweet, sassy personality and just pops with life. She has grown up at the shelter, but she will take full advantage of any opportunities to get out and meet people and mingle. She loves going on outings and does well with our other puppies.

Fiona

Akita | Age 4 | Female

Fiona is a shy girl and does best with another dog to lean on and show her the ropes, but she has the biggest heart. Earning her trust is a worthwhile endeavor that is endlessly rewarding. While she's nervous meeting new people, each time she has shown us that she is getting more comfortable and just needs people who will be patient with her. Her favorite things are her blanket and hard nylon chew toys!

Smokey

American Staffordshire Terrier | Age 3 | Male

Smokey is the sweetest most cuddly baby who loves a good walk and game of chase. He's very treat motivated so training is a breeze!! He's truly discovered how wonderful his life can be and embraces every second. He's going to make someone the most amazing partner.

Chowder

Great Pyrenees | Age 2 | Male

Chowder is what we call a unicorn in the rescue world- he is dog, cat AND respectful kiddo friendly! He is a submissive and happy guy who would love a canine companion to keep showing him the ropes on how to be a dog.

Kainan

German Shepherd | Age 2 | Male

Meet Kainan! A beautiful two year old male with the most amazing eyes you have ever seen. We are seeking an experienced adopter for kainan. Kainan was rescued from a rural shelter and sadly we do not know what this poor boy has seen in his lifetime. He needs a knowledgeable and patient adopter to help guide him out of his shell. Most importantly provide love and safety.

Delilah

Golden Retriever | Age 2 | Female

Delilah is a typical Golden, loving, energetic, and needs some training. She walks well on a leash, is crate-trained, and is fully housebroken. Delilah loves to cuddle and be a loyal snuggle buddy. She also gets along well with the other dogs and a cat in her foster home.
